We are Artevia Studio.

A creative collective from Sikkim that blends art, storytelling, and innovation. From podcasts and films to design, education, and merchandise, we craft experiences that inspire, connect, and leave a lasting impact.

Meet the Visionaries

Sarup Tiwari

Founder & Creative Lead

An artist, animator, and entrepreneur passionate about building a sustainable creative ecosystem in Sikkim, empowering local ideas through art and media.

Sekhar Sharma

Co-founder

A compelling writer and storyteller, Sekhar brings literary depth to Artevia's creative projects, including authoring the book "Nisabdh."

What We Do

We craft experiences across various creative domains.

Media & Production

From poignant short films and engaging podcasts to dynamic ad reels, we bring ideas to life on screen and in sound.

Merchandise

We design and create meaningful products – from apparel to art prints and books – that carry the essence of our stories.

Education & Workshops

Fostering the next generation of creators through immersive workshops on filmmaking, storytelling, and creative arts.

Culture & Research

Diving deep into the cultural fabric of the Himalayas, documenting oral histories, traditions, and untold stories.
